Privacy Policy

Last updated June 10, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information FAAM ("we", "us") collects, how we use it, and your choices. By using FAAM (the "Service"), you agree to this policy.

The short version: we collect the minimum needed to run your account and AI features. We don't sell your data. Payment card details go straight to Stripe — we never see or store them. You can delete your account at any time.

1.Information we collect

  • Account: your username, password (stored only as a salted hash), and optional email.
  • Content you add: watchlist, portfolio positions, adviser profile, and chats you send to the AI.
  • Usage: AI feature usage we meter to apply plan limits and run the Service.
  • Payments: handled by Stripe. We receive your subscription status, not your card number.
  • Technical: a session cookie to keep you signed in, plus basic logs.

2.How we use information

We use your information to provide and secure the Service, generate AI responses, apply your plan's features and limits, process subscriptions, and communicate with you about your account.

3.AI processing

When you use AI features, your prompt and relevant market context are sent to our AI provider (OpenAI) to generate a response. Don't paste sensitive personal information into the AI. OpenAI's handling of that data is governed by its own policies.

4.Market data

Stock quotes and charts come from third-party market-data sources. Requesting a symbol sends that symbol to the data provider so we can show you its data.

5.How we share information

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with service providers that help us operate FAAM, including OpenAI (AI), Stripe (payments), our hosting provider, and — if you choose Google sign-in — Google. We may disclose information if required by law or to protect the Service.

6.Cookies

We use a single signed session cookie to keep you logged in. We don't use third-party advertising or tracking cookies.

7.Data retention & security

We keep your data while your account is active. Passwords are hashed (PBKDF2), and sessions are signed. No system is perfectly secure, but we take reasonable measures to protect your data. You can delete your account to remove your information.

8.Your rights

You may access, correct, or delete your account information. Depending on where you live, you may have additional rights (such as under GDPR or CCPA); contact us to exercise them.

9.Children

FAAM is not intended for anyone under 18, and we don't knowingly collect data from children.

10.Changes

We may update this policy. We'll change the "Last updated" date above and, for material changes, take reasonable steps to notify you.
